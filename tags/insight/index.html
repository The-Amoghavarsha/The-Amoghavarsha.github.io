Web 1.5

0001-01-01 :: Amoghavarsha
#insight 
What’s in a name? That which we call “Interactive web”, “Read-Write web” and some other names would serve a bit more for social media. Web 2.0 was a revolution in its own right. The window of opportunities and possibilities compounded after that, the present tech giants never looked back. I really love tech forums, IRC channels, mailing lists and jabber chats. Especially the ones that are focused on STEM (in a broader sense), devoid of political opinions and subjective interpretations; just normal people discussing logical solutions to abiotic systems that could impact the masses.
