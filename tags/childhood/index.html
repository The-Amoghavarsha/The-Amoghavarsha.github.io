Memories of childhood..things that were lost with the climate

0001-01-01 :: Amoghavarsha
#childhood 
Where should I begin? Did I have the best childhood in the world? I don’t think so, but the fact that I didn’t know anything about childhood or adulthood was a solace. “Knowing Nothing” could be a taboo in this generation. But knowing nothing and knowing that I don’t know anything is something that made me a care free child and I loved it. But it didn’t last for a very long time as I realized that I wanted to pass examinations to qualify for the next classes!
